NotLoader


NotLoader is an attempt to replicate grappling hooks and swinging in video games. Basically you just grapple around trying to gain points by hitting red goals scattered around the map.

Its a very simple game, and I'm not likely to update it, but it did teach me some helpful things. And I reckon I should at least post something.
